Channel Types — Key Differences

Standard Channel

  • Visibility All team members
  • Who can create Configurable (members/owners)
  • SharePoint site Shared team site
  • Guests Allowed (if tenant permits)
  • Moderation Supported

Private Channel

  • Visibility Only invited members
  • Max per team 30 private channels
  • SharePoint site Own separate site collection
  • Guests Allowed
  • Apps/Tabs Limited (no connectors)
  • Bots Not supported

Shared Channel

  • Visibility Invited members + external via B2B DC
  • Max per team 50 shared channels
  • SharePoint site Own separate site collection
  • External users B2B Direct Connect (no guest account)
  • Guests (B2B invite) NOT supported
  • Teams meetings Not supported in shared channels
Team Roles & Limits

Team Limits (2026)

  • Max members per team 25,000
  • Max channels (standard) 200 per team
  • Max private channels 30 per team
  • Max shared channels 50 per team
  • Max teams per user 1,000
  • Max owners per team 100

Team Roles

  • Owner Full control of team settings
  • Member Standard collaboration
  • Guest Limited — no org data access
  • Moderator role Control who posts in a channel

Messaging Policy Settings

  • Delete sent messages Owner/member/off
  • Edit sent messages Configurable
  • Read receipts User-controlled/On/Off
  • Priority notifications E5 / Compliance add-on
  • URL previews Configurable
  • Giphy / memes / stickers Configurable per policy
App Management — Where to Configure

App Permission Policies

  • Purpose Control which apps users can install
  • Scope Microsoft / 3rd party / Custom apps
  • Where Teams Admin Center → Apps → Permission policies
  • Block specific app Block at org level in Manage apps

App Setup Policies

  • Purpose Pin apps to user's Teams left bar
  • Allow user pinning Configurable
  • Installed apps Pre-install apps for users
  • Where Teams Admin Center → Apps → Setup policies

Custom / LOB Apps

  • Upload method Teams Admin Center → Manage apps → Upload
  • Allow custom apps (org) Org-wide app settings toggle
  • Allow custom apps (user) App permission policy
  • Allow user upload (sideload) App setup policy setting
Teams Advisor & Frontline Workers

Advisor for Teams

  • Purpose Guided Teams rollout planning
  • Creates Deployment team + Planner tasks
  • Location Teams Admin Center → Planning → Advisor
  • Who uses it Teams admins planning new deployments

Frontline Teams

  • License M365 F1 / F3 (Frontline)
  • Feature Deploy to large frontline worker groups
  • Shift management Shifts app in Teams
  • Deploy via Teams Admin Center → Frontline deployment
